Villa Monge

Residential Interior Design Project — Switzerland, 2024

Villa Monge is a residential interior design project located in Switzerland. The main concept was to balance natural textures and soft lighting with contemporary functionality.

The proposal integrates locally sourced materials and a spatial flow that encourages connection between interior and exterior spaces.
